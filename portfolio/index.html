About Me

I began programming in 2020, starting with small Python projects and web tools. Soon after, I discovered my passion for building websites and shifted my focus to JavaScript. I explored this world through personal projects, games, animations, and useful tools for myself and others.

My background, however, is in the arts. I studied piano for 15 years, completing conservatory training before earning a bachelor’s degree in philosophy with a thesis on Zen and Japanese aesthetics. This education sharpened my appreciation for creativity, simplicity, and clarity — values that strongly influence both my design and my code.

I believe in building clean, intuitive interfaces without unnecessary clutter, and I apply the same philosophy to my programming: striving for efficiency, readability, and maintainability. For me, coding is a deeply creative process, one where solving problems is just as rewarding as the finished product.

I also gained industry experience as an intern at an IT company, where I built web templates for the telecommunications provider A1 in Slovenia. That role gave me a solid foundation in practical web development while reinforcing my passion for bringing ideas to life through code.
