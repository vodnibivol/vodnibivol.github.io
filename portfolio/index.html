About Me

I began programming in 2020, starting with small Python projects and web-related tools. Soon after, I discovered my passion for building websites. I focused on JavaScript, creating personal projects, games, animations, and useful tools for myself and others.

My design philosophy centers on simplicity, clarity, and functionality. I’ve always preferred clean, intuitive interfaces over cluttered designs filled with unnecessary features. The same principles guide my code: I aim for efficiency, readability, and maintainability. I enjoy solving problems myself, so I haven’t relied on AI in my projects—I simply love the process of programming.

Below you’ll find a selection of my projects. Some are older experiments with rougher edges, while others are more recent and polished. Together, they show my growth and approach as a developer.
